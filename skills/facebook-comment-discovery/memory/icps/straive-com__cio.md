# cio

- Last updated: 2026-06-18
- Offer: https://www.straive.com/
- ICP: CIO

## What worked

- LinkedIn was able to surface strategic AI-governance and operationalization discussion for this ICP.
- Facebook company-page post discovery worked only as a seed audit, not as a discussion source.

## What drifted

- Facebook drifted into brand content with inaccessible or empty comment surfaces.
- generic enterprise AI event promotion is not predictive enough for this buyer lane.

## Best predictive signals

- first-person operationalization, governance, or implementation discussion
- buyer-side comments about adoption risk, workflow change, and enterprise execution tradeoffs

## Strong role signals

- CIO
- chief information officer
- enterprise technology leader

## False-positive role signals

- broad AI evangelist
- event speaker
- vendor-side enterprise AI marketer

## Best queries

- AI governance, pilot-to-production, data readiness, and operationalization phrasing

## Weak queries

- broad enterprise AI event or summit promotion
- company-page post scraping without a visible public discussion surface

## Tuning rule

For this exact offer and ICP, prioritize strategic operator discussion over generic enterprise AI visibility. Facebook only stays in the mix if it can be seeded from community pages whose comments are visibly public and substantive.
